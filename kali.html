Kali

spring-summer 2019



Inspired by the art of Japanese flower arrangement, Ikebana, my second collection came to life in an array of delicate layers and diaphanous materials.


The fascinating discipline of guiding stem, flower, and leaf into a work of art, all the while following the unyielding rules of proportion, harmony, and focus, is resonant in the careful construction of the garments.


Gauzy overlays placed over crisscrossing patterns in soft silks and cottons are reminiscent of blooms - a celebration of grace and beauty and blossoming.
