Ajna

spring-summer 2018



Ajna, which means "to perceive" in Sanskrit, is the name of the chakra located between the eyes. Much like this chakra of inspiration and imagination, this is a collection of garments that speaks of my journey of self-discovery.


From the clean, crisp lines to the precise details, from the simple fabrics to the soothing colour, this collection of garments allows me to bring a part of myself to you.
