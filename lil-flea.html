The Lil Flea

06-08 April, 2018 | Mumbai, India

Padma was part of the Lil Flea in 2018, an event that showcases a collection of bohemian designers, artists, and trendsetters. With a growing community of over 400 lifestyle brands, live showcases, and creative ventures, it was the perfect platform for a new brand to showcase its collection.
