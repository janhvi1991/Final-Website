PADMA

The Label




Inspired from Hindu and Buddhist symbology of the lotus, Padma (the sanskrit word for 'lotus') is created for women who are strong and simple. Entrenched in Indian tradition, while soaking up minimalism from Japanese aesthetics, each design is meticulously handcrafted and well-thought out. The label strives to create designs that evolve with time, yet remain timeless; designs that hold special secrets that unravel each time the wearer puts them on.

Padma was launched in 2018 by Janhvi Sampat. This is an archival page for the brand.
