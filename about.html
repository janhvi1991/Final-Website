Designing. Learning. Living.

Hi there, I'm Janhvi - a multi-disciplinary designer, product- and operations-manager, and amateur illustrator!

Before moving to Copenhagen, I worked in Mumbai for six years with various firms like Payal Khandwala, Kizo, and Rewear . I also founded and managed a womenswear label.

Since I came to Copenhagen (December, 2021), I have been exploring the UI/UX design space independently. I learnt various design philosophies, technical aspects, and software related to UX design, and hope to expand my knowledge in the area with formal education and training.

Outside of design, I enjoy learning about new cultures and food, exploring the great outdoors, and petting every dog I see!

I'm always looking for new opportunities and collaborations.
